About me, Ron Bates, owner of Whitehall Digital, LLC

With a bachelors degree in mechanical engineering from WPI, a masters degree in computer science from Boston University, over 16 years of software application development at DS SolidWorks Corporation, and a passion for CG, I bring a diverse skill-set to any project that comes my way. Creativity balanced with logical thinking, along with a “get-it-done” attitude, to produce high quality renderings, animations, and customized software solutions that meet your business needs. From high quality still renderings of CAD data, to photo-realistic animations of complex machinery. From simple tools that play video files, to complex SolidWorks add-ins that automate your processes. You get a well rounded professional “artist-developer” to realize your needs. A very deliberate teacher, I’ve presented on graphics and rendering at SolidWorks user group meetings. I’ve also been a regular speaker at SolidWorks World events over the years, presenting and helping other present on rendering and animation topics. Feel free to contact me if you’re looking for a speaker/presentation at an event of yours.
